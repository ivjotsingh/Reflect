Therapy Getting Started With Therapy One of the most difficult parts of therapy is getting started.

Finding a therapist who is right for you can be tricky, and may take some time and effort until you find the right one.

And many people don't have access to in-person therapy options in convenient locations.

There are many things to consider as you get started, but we are here to help you navigate all of your most pressing questions about getting started with therapy.
